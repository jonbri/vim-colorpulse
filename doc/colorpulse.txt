INTRODUCTION                                    *colorpulse*

Prints out a word. For debugging.

Invoke with either |:ColorPulsePrev| / |:ColorPulseNext| or ]c / [c.

                                                *]c*
Invoke previous colorscheme
                                                *[c*
Invoke next colorscheme
